# Persona 2 — “Community-Minded Experienced Player”

## Persona Summary
- **Name:** Alex  
- **Age:** 22  
- **Occupation:** Part-time worker / recent graduate  
- **Gaming Profile:** Experienced player, enjoys optimisation and helping others  
- **Devices / Platform:** PC (primary), Console (secondary)  
- **Goal:** Share useful knowledge, support newer players, and feel recognised for contributions  

### Background
Alex has been playing a wide range of games for several years and is comfortable with complex mechanics, builds, and strategies. Over time, Alex has often helped friends and online community members by explaining systems, recommending approaches, and troubleshooting difficult sections of games.  

However, Alex finds that many existing platforms make it difficult to share concise, well-structured advice. Useful tips are often buried in long forum threads or lost in comment sections, reducing their impact and visibility.

---

## Goals
- Share clear, practical tips that genuinely help other players.
- Organise knowledge in a way that is easy for beginners to understand.
- Gain recognition for helpful contributions.
- Engage in a positive, cooperative gaming community.

---

## Pain Points
- Existing platforms prioritise popularity over usefulness.
- Helpful advice is often scattered across forums and comment threads.
- Repeating the same explanations to different people is time-consuming.
- Limited feedback on whether shared advice is actually useful.

---

## Motivations
- Enjoys mentoring and supporting less experienced players.
- Values structured problem-solving and clear explanations.
- Feels motivated when contributions are acknowledged by others.
- Wants to contribute to a respectful, non-toxic community environment.

---

## Typical Scenario (User Journey)
Alex encounters a recurring question about a difficult game mechanic.
They log into the platform and create a new Tip linked to the relevant game and challenge.
Alex structures the tip into clear steps with explanations and examples.
Other users engage with the tip, providing feedback or ratings.
Alex reviews their User Profile to track contributions and continue sharing knowledge.

---

## Opportunities for Design
- Easy-to-use tip creation with structured input fields.
- Clear association between tips, games, and problem types.
- Feedback mechanisms such as ratings or helpfulness indicators.
- User profiles that highlight contributions and community impact.
